How To Play

When you click the 'Play' button, the computer will randomly select a number between 1 and 10. Ten buttons will appear on the screen for you to choose the number you think the computer selected. If you guess correctly, you earn 5 points. Click the arrow button on the screen to view your stats, including total plays, correct guesses, wrong guesses, and your score.
